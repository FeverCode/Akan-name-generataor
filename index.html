Hello welcome to Akan name generator

Akan (Twi, Asante) names are the names used by the Akan people of Ghana and the Ivory Coast, who are frequently naming their baby boys and baby girls after the day of the week in which they were born and the order of birth for each of them, like first born Píèsíe, second born for baby boy Mǎnu/ for baby girl Máanu etc. See also, African origin baby names for more information about Akan boy names and Akan girl names and Ghanaian names to learn more about Ghanaian name meanings.

Akan name generator takes a user's birthday on the form provided and calculates the day of the week they were born and then depending on their gender outputs their Akan Name. This app will take in a user's year of birth, month of birth, day of birth and select gender. Using a formula assigned, it will be possible to determine the exact day of the week the user was born and give them an Akan name based on their gender.
